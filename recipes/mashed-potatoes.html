How to make Mashed Potatoes

Mashed Potatoes are a classic comfort food.

Let's make restauraunt quality mashed potatoes at home!

Ingredients

  • baking potatoes, peeled
  • garlic cloves, peeled
  • milk
  • butter
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil, then add the potatoes and garlic. Simmer until potatoes are soft.
  2. Heat milk and butter in a smaller saucpan under butter is melted
  3. Drain potatoes and grab a masher. Mash until potatoes are creamy and smooth.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
