How to make chicken noodle soup

Chicken noodle soup is a classic comfort food.

Instead of having it out of a can, let's make our own!

Ingredients

  • Chopped onion
  • Chopped celery
  • Sliced carrots
  • Egg noodles
  • Chicken broth
  • Vegetable broth
  • Chopped cooked chicken breast
  • Dried basil
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Melt butter in pot, add onion and celery.
  2. Add all other ingredients into pot. Stir and combine until brought to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for about 20 minutes.
